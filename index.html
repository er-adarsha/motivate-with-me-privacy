Privacy Policy for Motivate With Me

Effective Date: August 3, 2025

Welcome to Motivate With Me. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our app to manage and automate your social media content.

We may collect:

To:

All personal data is securely stored and encrypted. You can revoke access from social platforms at any time.

4. Third-Party Integrations

This app integrates with Facebook/Instagram, YouTube, and TikTok. Please review their respective privacy policies.
